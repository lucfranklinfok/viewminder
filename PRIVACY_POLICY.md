# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 22, 2025

## Introduction

ViewMinder ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you book our service, we collect:
- **Contact Information**: Full name, email address, mobile phone number
- **Booking Details**: Property address, inspection date/time, property listing URL
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely by Stripe (we do not store card details)

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent on site
- **IP Address**: For security and fraud prevention

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Services**
   - Attend property inspections on your behalf
   - Create and deliver video walkthroughs and reports
   - Communicate with property agents
   - Process payments

2. **Communication**
   - Send booking confirmations
   - Provide service updates
   - Respond to inquiries
   - Send important service notifications

3. **Improvement**
   - Improve our service quality
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Develop new features

4. **Legal Compliance**
   - Comply with applicable laws
   - Prevent fraud and abuse
   - Enforce our Terms of Service

## Information Sharing

### We Share Information With:

1. **Service Providers**
   - Stripe (payment processing)
   - Vercel (hosting)
   - Email service providers

2. **Property Agents**
   - Only the information necessary to register your interest
   - Typically: your name and contact details

### We Do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your information for marketing purposes
- Disclose your information except as described in this policy

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
- Secure data storage with Vercel
- PCI-compliant payment processing via Stripe
- Regular security audits

## Data Retention

We retain your information:
- **Active Bookings**: Until service is completed
- **Completed Bookings**: 12 months for support and record-keeping
- **Payment Records**: 7 years (tax compliance)

## Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

1. **Access**: Request a copy of your personal information
2. **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate information
3. **Deletion**: Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
4. **Object**: Object to processing of your information
5. **Portability**: Request transfer of your information

### How to Exercise Your Rights

Email us at: privacy@viewminder.com.au

We will respond within 30 days.

## Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies:
- **Essential Cookies**: Required for service functionality
- **Analytics**: To understand how our service is used (aggregated data only)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for persons under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
