# Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** November 22, 2025

## ViewMinder Confidence Guarantee

At ViewMinder, we stand behind our service with a 100% satisfaction guarantee.

## Automatic Full Refund Situations

You will receive an **immediate 100% refund** if:

1. **We miss your inspection**
   - Our agent fails to attend the scheduled inspection
   - We arrive late and the inspection has concluded

2. **We fail to deliver promised materials**
   - Video walkthrough is not delivered (for Standard/Premium tiers)
   - 15-point quality report is not delivered (for Standard/Premium tiers)
   - Photos are not delivered (all tiers)
   - Cover letter is not delivered to agent (Premium tier only)

3. **We fail to register your interest**
   - We do not check in with the property agent
   - Your name is not added to the application list

## Refund Request Process

### How to Request a Refund

1. Email: support@viewminder.com.au
2. Include:
   - Your booking reference number
   - Property address
   - Reason for refund request
3. We will respond within 24 hours

### Refund Timeline

- **Approval**: Within 24 hours of valid request
- **Processing**: 5-10 business days to appear in your account
- **Method**: Refund issued to original payment method via Stripe

## Partial Refund Situations

You may be eligible for a **partial refund** if:

- Service quality does not meet stated standards
- Delays in delivery (beyond 24 hours for Standard/Premium)
- Technical issues with video quality (case-by-case basis)

Partial refund amounts are determined on a case-by-case basis.

## No Refund Situations

Refunds will **not** be provided for:

1. **Client-caused issues**
   - Incorrect inspection details provided
   - Inspection cancelled by property agent
   - Client fails to respond to our communications

2. **Change of mind**
   - After we have successfully attended the inspection
   - After materials have been delivered as promised

3. **External factors**
   - Property already leased before inspection
   - Application rejected by agent/landlord
   - Agent cancels or reschedules inspection last minute

4. **Successful service delivery**
   - We attended the inspection as scheduled
   - All promised materials were delivered
   - Service was completed as described

## Rescheduling

If you need to reschedule:
- **More than 24 hours notice**: Free rescheduling
- **Less than 24 hours notice**: $20 rescheduling fee
- **Same day**: No rescheduling available, refund policy applies

## Disputed Charges

If you see an unexpected charge from ViewMinder:
1. Contact us first: support@viewminder.com.au
2. We will investigate and resolve within 24 hours
3. If unresolved, you may dispute through your bank

## Changes to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes will be posted on our website with an updated "Last Updated" date.
